A Clear Path from Soil to Shelf

Follow the journey of produce through our secure and transparent process.

1

Farmer Lists Produce

A farmer records a new harvest on the blockchain, detailing crop type, quantity, and expected price.

2

Authenticity Verified

A verifier confirms the farmer's credentials, creating the first block of trust in the chain.

3

Distributor Purchase

A distributor securely purchases the verified batch, with the payment transaction logged immutably.

4

Quality Certified

An authorized agency inspects the produce and certifies its quality and fair market price on the ledger.

5

Retailer Stocks

The retailer purchases the certified goods, and the final QR code is generated for consumer packaging.

6

Consumer Scans

The consumer scans the QR code in-store to instantly access the product's full, verified history.
